Frequently asked questions

Can't find your answer here? Message us on WhatsApp and we'll get back to you.

Do you take custom cake orders?

Yes! Message us on WhatsApp with your occasion, flavor preference, and date, and we'll confirm a design and price with you.

How far in advance should I order?

Same-day orders are welcome before 2 PM for most items, subject to availability. Custom cakes and large sets need at least 1 day's notice.

Do you offer halal-friendly ingredients?

We use trusted local suppliers for our ingredients. Please message us if you have specific dietary questions before ordering.

Can I pick up my order instead of delivery?

Yes, choose self-pickup at checkout and collect your order at our cottage in Merbok, Sungai Petani, free of charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept DuitNow QR / bank transfer and Touch 'n Go e-wallet. Payment is confirmed after you place your order.

Do you run baking workshops for beginners?

Yes, our workshops are beginner-friendly and hands-on. Check our Workshops page or book a slot through our workshop form.
