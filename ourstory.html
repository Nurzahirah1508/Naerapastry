The story behind our cottage oven

From weekend home baking to a daily cottage pantry — here's how Naera Pastry came to be.

How it began

A cottage kitchen, a full oven, and a lot of heart

Naera Pastry started in a small home kitchen in Merbok, Sungai Petani, where weekend baking for family and neighbors slowly turned into daily orders. What began as a handful of macarons shared with friends grew into a full pantry of tarts, croissants, scones, and cozy drinks — always made the same way we did on day one: fresh, buttery, and unhurried.

We named it after the idea of a "hearth" — a warm center of a home. Every box that leaves our cottage oven carries a little of that warmth with it.
