How delivery works at Naera Pastry

Everything you need to know about getting your order — delivered to your door or picked up fresh from our cottage.

Getting your order to you

Delivery information

We currently arrange delivery directly over WhatsApp, so fees and timing are confirmed with you before your order is baked.

🚚

Delivery area

We deliver around Merbok and greater Sungai Petani. Outside this area, we'll check if delivery is possible on request.

💰

Delivery fee

Fees are calculated by distance and confirmed with you on WhatsApp before payment, shown as "Calculated via WA" at checkout.

🏪

Self-pickup

Prefer to swing by? Choose self-pickup at checkout and collect your order at our cottage in Merbok, free of charge.

⏰

Order lead time

Same-day orders are welcome before 2 PM, subject to availability. Cakes and large sets need at least 1 day's notice.

📦

Packaging

Every order is boxed to keep pastries upright and drinks sealed, so everything arrives looking as good as it tastes.

💳

Payment

We accept DuitNow QR / bank transfer and Touch 'n Go e-wallet, confirmed once your order is placed.
